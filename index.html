ABOUT US

At P&M Prints, we believe every problem has a printable solution. Founded by two friends from Miami, Pablo and Michael, our store blends creativity, practicality, and passion to bring you high-quality 3D printed products and custom services.

Whether you're looking for ready-made items that solve everyday challenges or want something totally unique, we’ve got you covered.

Our mission is simple: “3D Printing Solutions To Every Problem.” From clever gadgets to one-of-a-kind creations, we use cutting-edge printing and thoughtful design to turn ideas into reality.

Beyond the shop, we’re building a community through our fun and educational 3D printing videos—sharing the process, the fails, the wins, and the “why didn’t I think of that?” moments that make this tech so exciting. With a growing online presence consisting of over 100,000 views across our social platforms, we’re not just printing—we’re inspiring.
